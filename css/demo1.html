Attack on Titan is an anime set in a world where humans live inside huge walls to stay safe from giant man-eating creatures called Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager, who loses his mother during a Titan attack and joins the military to fight them. Along with his friends Mikasa and Armin, he enters the Survey Corps to explore outside the walls and uncover the truth about the Titans. The anime is filled with action, mystery, emotions, and dramatic twists, showing a cruel world where people struggle for freedom and survival.

Death Note is an anime about a brilliant student named Light Yagami who finds a mysterious notebook that can kill anyone whose name is written in it. He decides to use the notebook to eliminate criminals and create a perfect world, calling himself “Kira.” However, his actions attract the attention of a world-famous detective known as L, leading to a tense battle of intelligence between the two. The story explores themes of justice, power, and morality, showing how absolute power can change a person’s mind and destiny.

DEMON SLAYERChapter 1 of Demon Slayer begins with Tanjiro Kamado, a kind boy who lives peacefully with his family and sells charcoal to earn money. One day, when he returns home from town, he finds his entire family killed by demons. The only survivor is his sister Nezuko, but she has turned into a demon. Even though she becomes dangerous, she still shows signs of protecting Tanjiro instead of hurting him. A demon slayer named Giyu Tomioka appears and tries to kill Nezuko, but after seeing her protect Tanjiro, he spares her life and tells Tanjiro to find a way to cure her. This marks the beginning of Tanjiro’s journey to become a demon slayer and save his sister.

JUJUTSU KAISENChapter 2 of Jujutsu Kaisen continues with Yuji Itadori after he swallows the cursed object — one of Sukuna’s fingers. Sukuna, the strongest curse, takes over Yuji’s body for a short moment, showing terrifying power. However, Yuji surprisingly manages to regain control and suppress Sukuna from inside, shocking everyone. Megumi Fushiguro and his teacher, Satoru Gojo, discover Yuji’s unusual ability to contain Sukuna. Instead of executing him immediately, Gojo suggests a risky plan — Yuji should join Tokyo Jujutsu High and collect all of Sukuna’s fingers before being executed. Yuji agrees, wanting to save people from curses and give others a chance to live.
